1. Medical Doctors

General Practitioners (GPs) / Family Physicians – primary care, general health issues

Specialists:

Cardiologists – heart & vascular system

Endocrinologists – diabetes, thyroid, hormonal disorders

Gastroenterologists – digestive system

Neurologists – brain & nervous system

Oncologists – cancer care

Pulmonologists – respiratory conditions

Nephrologists – kidney health

Dermatologists – skin, hair, nails

Psychiatrists – mental health, medication management

Pediatricians – child health

Obstetricians & Gynecologists (OB/GYN) – women’s reproductive health, pregnancy

Orthopedic Surgeons – bones, joints, muscles

Ophthalmologists – eye health & surgery

Otolaryngologists (ENT Specialists) – ear, nose, throat

Urologists – urinary system, male reproductive health

2. Nursing Professionals

Registered Nurses (RNs) – general care, patient support

Specialist Nurses:

Pediatric nurses

Surgical/Operating room nurses

ICU/critical care nurses

Oncology nurses

Community health nurses

3. Allied Health Professionals

Pharmacists – medication dispensing, drug safety, counseling

Physiotherapists – rehabilitation, mobility therapy

Occupational Therapists – daily activity support, rehabilitation

Nutritionists / Dietitians – meal planning, chronic disease diets

Radiographers / Imaging Technicians – X-ray, MRI, ultrasound operations

Laboratory Technologists – diagnostics, blood tests, microbiology

Speech & Language Therapists – communication/swallowing disorders

4. Mental & Behavioral Health

Psychologists – therapy, counseling (non-medical)

Counselors / Therapists – trauma, family therapy, addiction counseling

Social Workers – community-based health and social support

5. Dentistry & Oral Health

General Dentists – oral exams, fillings, preventive care

Orthodontists – braces, jaw alignment

Oral Surgeons – extractions, jaw surgery

Periodontists – gum health

Prosthodontists – dentures, implants

6. Traditional & Alternative Practitioners (depending on regulation in your country)

Herbal medicine practitioners

Acupuncturists

Chiropractors
